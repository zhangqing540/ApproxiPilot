# DSE

The `dse` component serves as the design space exploration module of the ApproxPilot framework. It is responsible for multi-objective optimization and automated exploration of approximate computing design configurations.

This component takes the predictions generated by the trained graph neural network models and explores the candidate design space to identify Pareto-optimal solutions under multiple objectives, including:

- **Area**
- **Power**
- **Latency**
- **SSIM**

## Role in ApproxPilot

Within the ApproxPilot framework, the `dse` component supports efficient search over approximate design candidates and enables Pareto-front construction for multi-objective optimization.
